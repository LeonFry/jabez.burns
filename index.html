Patented Perforated Drum

Our proprietary perforated drum gives you something solid drum roasters can’t—a convective roast. Beans are heated by the airflow through the drum rather than the drum itself, resulting in better heat transfer through the bean and a more even roast. The perforated design also means greater batch size flexibility.

Jabez Burns was an industry hero. The Jabez Burns brand is his legacy.

If you know coffee roasting history, you know the Jabez Burns name. In a story fit for the silver screen, Jabez Burns rose from humble teenage immigrant to American success story in just a few short decades. The coffee roaster he invented in the mid-1800s transformed the industry—opening the door to commercialization when it was purchased by the first grocer in North America ever to sell pre-roasted coffee.
